A local-first daily notes app — a per-day markdown editor with a calendar and
inline time logging. All data is stored in the browser (localStorage).

## Stack

Vue 3 · Vite · [Milkdown](https://milkdown.dev/) (Crepe editor)

## Try it

The app runs entirely in your browser at
**[hamesh.dev](https://hamesh.dev/)** — no account, no server, your notes never
leave your device.
